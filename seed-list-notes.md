# Seed List Notes

Created an initial seed list of 25 Western U.S. executive retreat venue candidates.

## Selection logic
Included venues that appear to satisfy most of these:
- executive-appropriate positioning
- lodging on site
- meeting/offsite suitability
- premium or upper-midmarket feel
- strong setting (mountain, vineyard, coast, desert, forest)
- useful for leadership retreats rather than mass-market conferences

## Important caveat
This is a candidate seed list, not a fully verified production dataset.
Some rows are based on reputable secondary roundup pages and still need direct venue-site verification for:
- meeting capacity
- lodging capacity
- buyout availability
- nearest airport / drive time
- executive-specific fit

## Next verification pass
For each seed venue, verify directly from venue site:
1. meetings / retreats page exists
2. lodging on site exists
3. likely airport and drive-time estimate
4. whether venue feels suitable for executive / leadership retreats
5. notes on differentiators

## Immediate recommendation
Narrow the actual launch list to the first 12-15 strongest venues after direct verification.
