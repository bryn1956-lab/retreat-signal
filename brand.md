# Brand Direction

## Working project name
Executive Retreat Directory

## Better brand candidates
1. Westward Retreats
2. Offsite West
3. Summit Ledger
4. Boardroom & Bonfire
5. West Exec Retreats
6. Leadership Lodgings
7. Retreat Signal
8. Cinder & Cedar Retreats

## Recommendation
Use **Retreat Signal** as the working brand.

Why:
- sounds focused and selective
- implies high-signal curation over generic travel discovery
- flexible enough to expand into concierge and premium placement
- not trapped in a single geography forever

## Positioning line
Curated executive retreat venues for leadership teams in the Western U.S.

## Voice
- concise
- premium but not fluffy
- practical
- operator-oriented
- planner-friendly
