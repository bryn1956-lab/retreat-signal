# Positioning

## Chosen direction
- Region: Western U.S.
- Audience: executives
- Build preference: cheapest practical path that still yields a credible asset

## Initial positioning statement
A curated directory of executive retreat venues in the Western U.S. for leadership offsites, strategy retreats, and small executive team gatherings.

## Who it is for
- founders
- leadership teams
- executive assistants
- chiefs of staff
- people ops / team leads planning small high-value offsites

## Why they would use it
Because generic travel sites are noisy and retreat-planning agencies are expensive or overkill for initial discovery.

## Core promise
Find executive retreat venues with the filters decision-makers actually care about:
- easy airport access
- lodging on site
- meeting space
- buyout suitability
- privacy
- setting
- team size fit

## Wedge
Start with the Western U.S. and venues that support both lodging and executive meeting needs.

## Anti-positioning
Not:
- a generic travel blog
- a broad vacation rental directory
- a giant global retreat marketplace on day one
